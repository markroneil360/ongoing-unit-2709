Method and interpretation

What this dashboard establishes

  • The public peer aggregate provides an independent comparison environment built from 27 HDF and 22 EHZ stations.
  • HDF pressure and EHZ ground motion are processed and interpreted separately.
  • The displayed record can establish timing, relative magnitude, continuity, and differences from the peer reference.
  • The station alone does not identify a person, device, building system, propagation path, intent, or medical effect.
Common-sense reading: the measured R6E8A environment is materially elevated in the cited comparisons relative to the independent peer aggregate. Detailed source attribution requires synchronized additional sensors and professional acoustic or structural investigation.
