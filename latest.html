Method and interpretation

What this dashboard establishes

  • Scores use the available R6E8A archive; missing dates are excluded and shown as N/A.
  • HDF infrasound/pressure and EHZ vertical motion remain separate evidence streams.
  • 100% represents the best relative condition; 70% is the selected pass threshold.
  • The Estimated Indoor Noise Criteria Equivalent is an engineering estimate, not a formal calibrated NC rating.
